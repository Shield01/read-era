About Readera

This is a platform that allows its esteemed users unlimited access to books of all kinds. Readera was founded because the importance of the preserving the reading culture of the human race cannot be over-emphasised. Every product that we present to the public is not only directed to education but also the preservation of knowledge through information technology integration. Users are allowed access to a notepad that is backed up to a massive virtual storage, to aid presevation of knowledge.

The Vision

To disemminate knowledge to every human being, irrespective of age, tribe, religion or colour. Through the primary use of information technology.
